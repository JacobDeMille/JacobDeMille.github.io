Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help people who want to get started in quilting but don't know how to start.

Audience

Audience persona 1: a young girl, in her early to mid-teens. Someone who has seen her grandmother or mother do quilting and wants to learn but has no idea where to start. She could find a lot of use from the different how to videos. She could find simple patterns to try out first before she decides to go all in on a big quilt.
Audience persona 2: o Another persona would be a young adult/mom in their 20’s-early 30’s who doesn’t have a lot of time on their hands and would love to take up a new hobby but they don’t know where to start or how much it would cost. They could benefit greatly from the contact form to be able to ask some direct questions and find out exactly where to start.
